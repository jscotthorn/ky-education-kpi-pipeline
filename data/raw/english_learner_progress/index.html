🌐 English Learner Progress - Raw Data

English Language Proficiency: Original files from Kentucky Department of Education containing English learner progress and proficiency rates by school, district, and grade levels. Tracks student development toward English language proficiency.
  • 📄 KYRC24_ACCT_English_Learners_Progress_Proficiency_Rate.csv 2024
  • 📄 english_language_proficiency_2023.csv 2023
  • 📄 english_language_proficiency_2022.csv 2022

📊 Data Description

These files contain English learner progress data for Kentucky schools and districts. The data includes:

  • Progress rates - Students advancing toward English proficiency
  • Proficiency rates - Students achieving English language proficiency
  • Grade-level breakdowns - Elementary, middle, and high school rates
  • Demographic analysis - Progress by student subgroups

🔄 Processing Notes

  • Files are processed by etl/english_learner_progress.py
  • Original column names and formatting preserved
  • Standardized output available in data/processed/english_learner_progress.csv
  • Demographic mapping audit available in processed directory

Source: Kentucky Department of Education via Kentucky Report Card (KRC)
