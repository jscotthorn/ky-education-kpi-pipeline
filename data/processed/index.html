📁 ky-education-kpi-pipeline / data / processed
Processed Data: These files contain cleaned, standardized education metrics in KPI format. Demographics are mapped to ensure consistent naming across years and data sources while maintaining granular analysis capabilities. Each metric includes demographic audit logs that track all mapping transformations to ensure no inadvertent combining of demographics that should remain separate.

📊 Standardized Data Files

Cleaned education metrics in standardized 10-column KPI format with consistent demographic groupings across all years and data sources.

  • 📊 chronic_absenteeism.csv Student attendance patterns and chronic absenteeism rates
  • 📊 english_learner_progress.csv English language proficiency development rates
  • 📊 graduation_rates.csv 4-year high school graduation outcomes
  • 📊 kindergarten_readiness.csv School readiness screening composite scores
  • 📊 out_of_school_suspension.csv Student discipline and suspension rates
  • 📊 postsecondary_enrollment.csv College enrollment after high school graduation
  • 📊 postsecondary_readiness.csv College and career readiness indicators

📋 File Types
